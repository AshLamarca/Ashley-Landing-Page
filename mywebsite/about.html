About Me

Aloha! My name is Ashley Lamarca, born and raised on Oahu, Hawaii. In November of 2021, my very supportive husband and I made the decision to move to Las Vegas, starting a new venture. While the decision to leave family and friends behind was extremely difficult, we felt the change in economy was the best option to thrive in life.

While I'm constantly feeling homesick and it was difficult leaving again after our last visit home, moving was certainly the best decision. This may not be our forever home but it is certainly an enlightening experience.
